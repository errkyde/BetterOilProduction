# Better Oil Production

Tired of the vanilla pumpjack being the only option for your entire playthrough? **Better Oil Production** adds three new pumpjack tiers and — with Space Age — a full space-based oil synthesis chain so you can source crude oil even when you've left the planet behind.

---

## New Pumpjacks

**Better Pumpjack Mk2** — double the output of the vanilla pumpjack. A straightforward upgrade for mid-game oil expansion.

**Better Pumpjack Mk3** — four times the extraction rate. For when your oil demands outpace everything else.

**Eco-Friendly Pumpjack** — extracts at normal speed but with dramatically reduced resource depletion. Keep your oil fields alive indefinitely instead of watching them run dry.

All three machines are fast-replaceable with each other, so upgrading your existing fields is painless.

---

## Space Age: Oil from Asteroids

Running out of oil patches on Nauvis? Ship the problem to space.

Crushing **carbonic asteroids** now has a chance to drop **hydrocarbon chunks** alongside the normal carbon yield. Feed those chunks — along with water — into the new **Hydrocarbon Synthesizer** on your space platform to produce **synthetic crude**: a solid, rocketable item.

Fly it back to the planet, drop it in a chemical plant, and heat it. Out comes **500 crude oil per unit**. No extra ingredients needed on the planet side.

The Hydrocarbon Synthesizer supports productivity and speed modules, so it scales well with late-game infrastructure.

**Research:** Space Oil Synthesis (automation + logistic + chemical + space + metallurgic science packs)
