Version: 0.1.4
Date: 25.05.2026
  Features:
    - Space Age: Added space oil synthesis feature. Crushing carbonic asteroids now has a 30% chance to drop hydrocarbon chunks. A new Hydrocarbon Synthesizer (space platform machine) converts chunks and water into solid synthetic crude. Synthetic crude can be rocketed back to the planet and heated in a chemical plant to yield 500 crude oil per unit.
    - Space Age: Pumpjacks Mk2, Mk3 and Eco now require atmospheric pressure (planet-only) and have heating energy for cold-planet survival.
    - Space Age: Advanced Pumpjacks Mk3 and Eco Pumpjack technologies now require space-tier science packs.
    - Space Exploration: Advanced Pumpjacks Mk3 and Eco Pumpjack technologies now require se-rocket-science-pack.
  Bugfixes:
    - Fixed pipe_connections using deprecated positions array format instead of single position field.
    - Fixed Mk3 collision box being smaller than its selection box.
    - Removed deprecated icon_mipmaps field from all item definitions.
    - Fixed technology locale keys to match actual technology names.
    - Fixed leading blank line in changelog.
